About bitcoin banks

Bitcoin banks are not registered banks and thus not allowed to call themselfs banks officially, but they act as a typical bank since they are a custodian of your bitcoins. The users are not in direct control of the bitcoins. If the service goes offline it is not possible to retrive the funds through technical means.

About web wallets

Web wallets are a convenient way to handle your bitcoins. Your bitcoins are handled directly in your browser and not handed over to a third party, but the security is only as good as the web browser you use. Beware of malware and viruses.

About desktop wallets

The original way of handling bitcoin. Desktop wallets are typical applications installed on your computer. In this category you will find the original bitcoin client and several other clients that has been around a long time.

About mobile wallets

Mobile wallets are the convenient way of using bitcoin. It enables users to quickly and easily spend bitcoins on the run. Make sure to backup your wallet in case you lose your phone.

About hardware wallets

Hardware wallets are the most secure way to use bitcoin. Your bitcoin keys are protected by custom hardware built with security in mind. They cost more than most wallets but might be a good investment if you need to secure a lot of bitcoins.
